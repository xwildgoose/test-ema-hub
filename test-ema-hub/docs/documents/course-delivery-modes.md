# Course Delivery Modes

How UB courses are delivered. (LMS = Learning Management System = Moodle.)

## Face-to-Face (FF) — 100% in person
A synchronous course where all instruction happens in a designated space at a scheduled
time, with instructor and students present. Work may be submitted in hard copy or online
via the LMS at the instructor's discretion.

## Hybrid (HY) — 50% in person, 50% online
Instruction is split equally between face-to-face and asynchronous online delivery via
the LMS.

## Flex — 100% online (50% synchronous, 50% asynchronous)
A remote course split equally between synchronous online instruction and asynchronous
online delivery via the LMS.

## Real Time (RT) — 100% synchronous online
A remote, synchronous course held in an online virtual classroom at a scheduled time,
with instructor and students present. Work is submitted online.

## Online (OL) — 100% asynchronous online
A remote, asynchronous course conducted entirely in the LMS — submissions,
communication, learning media and activities, tests, discussions, and instructor feedback
all happen online.

---

**Synchronous** = real-time interaction (like a live class where everyone participates at
once). **Asynchronous** = learning at different times (like watching a recorded lecture or
completing assignments on your own schedule).

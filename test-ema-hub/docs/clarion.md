### Private Awards and Scholarships Portal Now Open
The Financial Aid and Scholarships Department has opened the Private Awards and
Scholarships Portal. Students are encouraged to apply as early as possible.

**Application windows**

- First window: now through 24 July 2026
- Second window: 31 July through 31 August 2026

**How to apply**

1. Visit the UB website to review the list of available Private Awards and
   Scholarships (including eligibility and award details).
2. Log in to Banner.
3. Select **Financial Aid** and follow the prompts.

For assistance, contact Financial Aid and Scholarships at **fas@ub.edu.bs**.
